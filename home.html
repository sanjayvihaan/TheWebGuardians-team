About Team

The Web Guardians is an organization of five enthusiastic cyber safety advocates from East Point Group of Institutions, working to make the World Wide Web a safer and much better location. There are countless online threats occurring nowadays, loads of individuals fall prey to them and Privacy is our main motto. so we've developed a software program that finds phishing URLs typically distributed inside mails and messages and lots of other message systems for stealing user information and facts.
